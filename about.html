Our Mission

At Light of Glory Evangelical Church, our mission is to shine the light of Christ in our community and beyond. We are dedicated to spreading the Gospel, nurturing spiritual growth, and serving others with love and compassion.

We strive to be a beacon of hope and a place of refuge, where individuals can encounter God's love and find purpose in their lives. Together, we seek to build a vibrant community that reflects the grace and truth of Jesus Christ.

Join us as we walk in faith, empowering each other to live out our calling and make a positive impact in the world around us.

Our Ministries

At Light of Glory Evangelical Church, we are committed to serving our community and fostering spiritual growth through various ministries. Each ministry is designed to meet the needs of our members and the wider community, helping individuals grow in faith and service.

  • Worship Ministry: Leading congregational worship and creating an atmosphere of praise and adoration.
  • Children's Ministry: Providing a safe and nurturing environment for children to learn about God's love.
  • Youth Ministry: Empowering young people to develop their faith and engage with their peers through fun and impactful activities.
  • Outreach Ministry: Sharing the Gospel and serving the community through various service projects and events.

Join us as we explore the teachings of Christ! In a community filled with love and support, we come together to grow in faith, uplift one another, and share the light of His message.
