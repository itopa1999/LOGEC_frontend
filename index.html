Our Services

At Light of Glory Evangelical Church, we offer a welcoming space for worship and spiritual growth. Join us for uplifting services, heartfelt prayers, and engaging teachings that deepen your faith and strengthen our community.

Monday: Mother’s Prayers
5pm-6pm

Join us every Monday as mothers gather for a powerful hour of prayer and seeking God’s guidance and strength together.

Tuesday: Biblical Counseling
5pm-6pm

Join us every Tuesday for an hour of Biblical Counseling, where we explore God’s Word together and find answers to life’s challenges.

Wednesday: Prayer on the Mountain
9am - 12pm

Join us every Wednesday for "Prayer on the Mountain, a dedicated time of prayer and reflection. Experience the power of collective prayers

Thursday: Youth Programme
2pm - 4pm

Join us every Thursday for our Youth Programme, where young hearts come together for fellowship, fun, and spiritual growth. This is a great opportunity to build friendships, explore faith, and engage in meaningful discussions about life and God’s Word.

Friday: Night Vigil
12am - 3pm

Join us every Friday for our Night Vigil, a powerful time of prayer, worship, and spiritual renewal. Together, we seek God’s presence in the stillness of the night, interceding for our needs and those of our Church in a focused and intimate setting.

Sunday: Sunday School & Sunday Service
8am - 9am & 9am - 12pm

Join us every Sunday for a meaningful morning of worship and learning! Our Sunday School offers engaging lessons for all ages, followed by our Sunday Service where we come together to celebrate, worship, and grow in faith as a community.
